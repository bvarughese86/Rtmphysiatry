Physician Detail

RTM Physiatry & Psychiatry, PLLC, provides you with personalized, one-on-one care you deserve in a friendly and comfortable atmosphere. Our physician Thresiamma Mathew, M.D. is a Physiatrist who specializes in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation. After her graduation from TD Medical College, India, she completed her Internship in General surgery from Our Lady of Mercy Medical Center/New York Medical College and her Residency in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation from Metropolitan Hospital Center in New York Medical College. Currently, she works as an Attending Physician in the outpatient department of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation in Queens Hospital Center, affiliated with Mt. Sinai School of Medicine. The physician performs an Initial Evaluation through patient histories and a thorough physical examination, diagnose and treat injuries and conditions, and direct your expanded treatment using non-surgical methods.

About RTM Physiatry and Psychiatry PLLC

RTM Physiatry & Psychiatry, PLLC offers evaluation and treatment to our patients with pain and a wide range of injuries. We offer physical therapy for pre or post-operative orthopedic disorders, neck pain, back pain, multiple joint pain, sports injuries, tendinitis, bursitis, neurological disorders such as post stroke, neuropathy, Carpal Tunnel Syndrome, post amputation and trauma related injuries. We also offer Joint and Soft tissue injection, Nerve Conduction Studies and EMG, Orthotics & Prosthetics, Durable Medical Equipments (DME).

At RTM Physiatry & Psychiatry, PLLC, our goal is treating our patients with compassion and providing the best possible treatment and care that they deserve. You will be scheduled as soon as possible by our friendly receptionist. Currently, we are open on every Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, and Saturday, 9.00am-5.00pm.

Role of Physiatrist

Physiatrists focus on treating patients who are in pain or who have been disabled as a result of disease, condition, disorder, or injury, that leads to a personalized method of treatment to improve the patients' quality of life. As a result, patient's recuperation involves every aspect of their lives. Our Physician has more than 9 years of experience and is up to date with all current techniques and treatments. Our patients receive individual attention during each visit. Our goal is to restore each patient's highest possible level of function.
